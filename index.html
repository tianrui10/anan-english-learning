🌟 Day 1: Max and His Clever Dog
🐶
Leo is a clever dog. He lives with a young boy named Max in a small town. Every morning, Leo waits by the front door. When Max puts on his shoes, Leo wags his tail happily because he knows it is time for a walk. Today, instead of going to the park, Max takes Leo to a new place. There are many trees, a beautiful lake, and three little ducks swimming quietly. Leo looks at the ducks curiously, but he does not bark. He just sits by the lake and enjoys the warm sunshine with Max.
❓ Question 1: Why does Leo wag his tail happily every morning?
A. Because he sees three little ducks.
B. Because he knows it is time for a walk.
C. Because Max gives him a nice shoe.
解析：文中提到 Leo 看到 Max 穿鞋就知道要去散步了，所以高兴地摇尾巴。
❓ Question 2: Where do Max and Leo usually go for a walk on other days?
A. To the park.
B. To a school.
C. To a busy store.
解析：文中提到“Today, instead of going to the park...”，说明平时习惯去公园。
❓ Question 3: What does the word "curiously" mean in the story?
A. In a very angry way.
B. Wanting to know or see something.
C. Feeling very tired.
解析：“curiously” 意思是“好奇地”。看到新见到的鸭子，小狗感到很好奇。
❓ Question 4: What is True about Leo when he sees the ducks?
A. He jumps into the water to chase them.
B. He barks very loudly at them.
C. He stays quiet and sits down by Max.
解析：最后提到“but he does not bark. He just sits by the lake”，说明他安静地坐下了。
🌟 Day 2: The Secret of the Forest
🌳
Mia loves spending time outdoors. On a sunny afternoon, she walks into a deep forest behind her grandmother's house. The forest is quiet, and the air smells like fresh rain. Suddenly, Mia spots something shiny hidden under a large, old oak tree. She runs closer and discovers a small, wooden box wrapped in a green ribbon. With exciting hands, she opens it slowly. Inside the box, there is a beautiful stone that glows with a soft blue light and a short note that says: "To the person who loves nature." Mia smiles warmly and decides to keep this wonderful secret in her heart.
❓ Question 1: Where is the deep forest located?
A. Next to Mia's school.
B. Behind her grandmother's house.
C. Inside a big city park.
解析：开头明确写到“she walks into a deep forest behind her grandmother's house”。
❓ Question 2: What does Mia discover under the large oak tree?
A. A small wooden box with a ribbon.
B. A golden key to a house.
C. A little bird that cannot fly.
解析：文中提到“discovers a small, wooden box wrapped in a green ribbon”。
❓ Question 3: What is special about the stone inside the box?
A. It is very heavy and dark.
B. It glows with a soft blue light.
C. It is made of sweet chocolate.
解析：文中描述s石头是“a beautiful stone that glows with a soft blue light”。
❓ Question 4: What does the note in the box say?
A. "Find the gold here."
B. "To my dear grandmother."
C. "To the person who loves nature."
解析：纸条上的原文是："To the person who loves nature."。
🌟 Day 3: Ben's Special Star Project
🚀
Ben is eleven years old and loves science very much. This week, his school has a special project about space. Ben decides to make a model of the solar system. He uses colorful paper, small plastic balls, and a lot of silver glue to make the stars shine. His younger sister, Lily, wants to help him, so Ben asks her to paint the planet Mars bright red. They work together at the kitchen table for three evenings. On Friday morning, Ben carries his beautiful project safely to school. His teacher is proud and gives him a big shiny gold star.
❓ Question 1: What subject does Ben love very much?
A. History and Art.
B. Science.
C. Music.
解析：开头提到“loves science very much”，说明他最喜欢科学。
❓ Question 2: What does Ben ask his sister Lily to do?

C. Buy some new plastic balls.
解析：文中写道“Ben asks her to paint the planet Mars bright red”。
❓ Question 3: How long do they work on the star project?
A. For only one morning.
B. For three evenings.
C. For a whole month.
解析：文中提到“They work together at the kitchen table for three evenings”。
❓ Question 4: What reward does Ben get on Friday morning?

C. A big shiny gold star from his teacher.

🌟 Day 5: Grandfather's Magic Pocket Watch
⌚
Tom's grandfather has a beautiful golden pocket watch. It does not use batteries, but needs to be turned with a small key every night. Grandfather tells Tom that this watch is eighty years old and has traveled across many countries. When you put the watch close to your ear, it makes a steady "tick-tock" sound. To Tom, it sounds like a tiny heart beating. Grandfather promises to give this special watch to Tom on his twelfth birthday next year.
❓ Question 1: How does the pocket watch get its power to run?
A. It uses a modern computer battery.
B. It needs to be turned with a small key every night.
C. It gets power from the warm sun.

❓ Question 2: How old is the golden pocket watch?
A. Twelve years old.
B. Eighty years old.
C. One hundred years old.
解析：爷爷告诉 Tom 这块表已经80岁了（this watch is eighty years old）。
❓ Question 3: What does the steady "tick-tock" sound remind Tom of?
A. A tiny heart beating.
B. A loud bird singing.
C. A big heavy rain.
解析：对 Tom 而言，滴答声听起来像一颗微小的心脏在跳动（it sounds like a tiny heart beating）。
❓ Question 4: When will Tom receive this pocket watch as a gift?

解析：最后一句明确写到“promises to give this special watch to Tom on his twelfth birthday next year”。
